Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I purchase cards? A: Browse our eBay store or contact us through Instagram for custom orders! We accept PayPal, Venmo, Cash App, and Zelle.
Q: Are all cards authentic? A: Yes! We verify the authenticity of every card before listing. All high-value cards include authenticity photos.
Q: Do you ship internationally? A: Yes, we ship worldwide! Shipping costs vary by location. International orders typically take 2-3 weeks.
Q: Can I request specific cards? A: Absolutely! Contact us and we'll let you know if we have it in stock or can help you find it!
Q: What's your return policy? A: We accept returns within 7 days if the card doesn't match the description. Cards must be in original condition.
Q: Do you offer card grading services? A: We don't grade cards ourselves, but we can help you find reputable grading services. We also sell pre-graded cards!
Q: How do I track my order? A: You'll receive a tracking number via email or eBay message once your order ships. Most orders ship within 1-2 business days.
Q: Do you buy collections? A: Yes! If you have a collection to sell, contact us with photos and we'll make you an offer. We're always looking for new inventory!
